Last updated: July 2025. Applies to all Benevolence Inc apps on Android and iOS.

📢 Advertisement Privacy

This application uses Google Ad Services (AdMob) to display advertisements. We do not collect or store any personal data directly. However, as a third-party provider, Google may collect certain information to deliver relevant ads. For users under the age of 13, advertisements are configured to be child-friendly and non-personalized in accordance with COPPA and Google Play Families Policy.

🎙️ Microphone Permission

Our app may request microphone access for voice input or speech-to-text features. This is used only while actively using these features. We do not record, store, or share any audio data. Voice input is processed in real-time and not retained. The microphone is never accessed in the background. You can revoke access anytime via device settings.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our apps may be accessed by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If a child under 13 has submitted personal data, we will promptly delete it. Parents or guardians may contact us at benevolenceincindia@gmail.com to review or request deletion.

🔒 Data Safety

We do not collect personal data directly. Our apps may share Device IDs with Google AdMob for serving advertisements. All data transmitted is encrypted in transit. No account or login is required to use any of our apps.
