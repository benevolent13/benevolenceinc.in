Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2025. Applies to all Benevolence Inc apps.

📢 Advertisement Privacy

This application uses Google Ad Services (AdMob) to display advertisements. We do not collect or store any personal data directly. However, as a third-party provider, Google may collect certain information to deliver relevant ads. For users under the age of 13, advertisements are configured to be child-friendly and non-personalized in accordance with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play Families Policy. This means ads are based solely on general app content and not on personal data.

🎙️ Microphone Permission

Our app may request access to your device's microphone to enable features such as voice input or speech-to-text functionality. This permission is used only while you are actively using these features. We do not record, store, or share any audio data. Any voice input is processed in real-time and is not retained after use. The microphone is never accessed in the background without your knowledge. You can control or revoke microphone access at any time through your device settings.

👶 Children's Privacy

Our app is intended for a general audience and may be accessed by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child under 13 has submitted personal data, we will promptly delete such information. Parents or guardians may contact us at benevolenceincindia@gmail.com to review or request deletion of any information their child may have provided.

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at benevolenceincindia@gmail.com.
